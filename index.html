✩°｡ ⋆ About Me ⋆ ｡°✩

I'm Beatty Fulmina (he/they), an art generalist with experience in using a range of 2D and 3D processes to create animations, games, and illustrations.


Art to me is essentially people-watching; absorbing the breadth of audience responses, observing ways interactive systems can be broken, analyzing new works made in response to these circumstances, etc. all reveal key insights into the nature of humanity, especially in the digital age.


Thus, I enjoy accumulating new skills to gain a wider visual vocabulary for describing internal and external retrospection, with both the experience of learning and the resulting end products being valuable to that goal.
