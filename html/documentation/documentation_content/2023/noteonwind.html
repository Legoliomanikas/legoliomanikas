✩°｡ ⋆ a note on the wind ⋆ ｡°✩



Play on browser at itch.io
A thrown letter, in the form of a paper airplane, guided by the wind. Its destination is unknown, but certain.
You are not in control of the plane, instead, you take control of the wind: you are what is typically the obstacle and must guide the paper plane through the world.
