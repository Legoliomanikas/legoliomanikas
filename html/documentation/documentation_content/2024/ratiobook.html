✩°｡ ⋆ Dr. Ratio Business Card Holder ⋆ ｡°✩



Download on itch.io
Free 3D print file of a business card holder based on Dr. Ratio's weapon from Honkai: Star Rail.
Made with my personal use (and supplies) in mind, so requires additional materials such as bamboo skewers and elastic to assemble.

✩°｡ ⋆ ~ ⋆ ｡°✩


Capacity (per side) (assuming 14pt weight card):
47 (without chalk), 14 (with chalk)


✩°｡ ⋆ ~ ⋆ ｡°✩


✩°｡ ⋆ Process: ⋆ ｡°✩

I took the base model from Dr. Ratio's official MMD, and stretched it to more box-like dimensions. Booleans were used to cut out space for the hinges.


Besides the functional changes needed to make it into a box, I also added additional geometry to the parts of the model that were detailed only through textures.



I used marble-effect PLA for the print, metallic paints for the gold, and magnetic nail polish for the purple and blue parts.
The latter gives off a chatoyant/anisotrophic effect that is difficult to catch on camera.



The hinges were made via a bamboo skewer fed through holes connecting the spine to the covers.
The magnets I used for the closure were quite strong; I had to reglue them a few times after carrying the box around, so perhaps slightly weaker magnets would be better, especially since the elastics would keep the internal contents safe regardless.
